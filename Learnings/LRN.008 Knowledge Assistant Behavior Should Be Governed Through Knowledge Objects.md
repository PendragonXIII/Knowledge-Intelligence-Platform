# LRN.008 Knowledge Assistant Behavior Should Be Governed Through Knowledge Objects

## Standard

[[KTS.002 Learning Standard]]

## Learning

Knowledge Assistant behavior becomes more maintainable, reviewable and reusable when review procedures and assessment logic are represented as Knowledge Objects rather than embedded directly in GPT instructions.

GPT instructions should remain lightweight and primarily provide identity, bootstrap guidance and repository access patterns.

Repeatable reasoning workflows should be documented as versioned Knowledge Objects.

## Evidence

During Knowledge Repository development, review procedures evolved through repeated assessment sessions.

The resulting review logic proved valuable beyond individual conversations and was formalized as a reusable Playbook.

This approach enabled governance, review and continuous improvement of the process itself.

## Impact

Improves:

- maintainability
- governance consistency
- AI transparency
- process reuse
- continuous improvement
