## Observation

Traditional documentation focuses on files and folders.

AI reasoning benefits more from structured knowledge objects and explicit relationships between them.

## Learning

Knowledge should primarily be modeled as:

- Capabilities
- Constraints
- Opportunities
- Playbooks
- Learnings
- Decisions

rather than as isolated documents.

## Implications

Future knowledge management should prioritize:

- relationships over folders
- discoverability over hierarchy
- context over documentation volume
