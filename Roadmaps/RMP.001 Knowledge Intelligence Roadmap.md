## Status

Active

## Purpose

Provide a structured evolution path for EID.08 Knowledge Intelligence.

The roadmap defines the recommended implementation sequence and ensures that Knowledge Intelligence evolves through incremental, reusable capabilities rather than isolated initiatives.

## Vision

Create an AI-consumable knowledge layer that captures engineering reasoning, preserves institutional knowledge and improves AI-assisted engineering workflows.

---

# RMP.001 Knowledge Intelligence Roadmap

## Phase 1 – Knowledge Foundation

### Status

In Progress

### Objective

Establish the initial knowledge model, governance structure and knowledge graph.

### Exit Criteria

- CNS.005 Knowledge Object Standards exists
- PLB.003 Knowledge Object Creation exists
- Capability Template exists
- Opportunity Template exists
- Constraint Template exists
- Learning Template exists
- ADR Template exists
- Playbook Template exists
- Roadmap Template exists
- All EID capabilities use the approved capability schema
- Every knowledge object contains at least three relationships

---

## Phase 2 – Knowledge Generation

### Status

Not Started

### Objective

Create a repeatable process for converting AI sessions into structured knowledge.

### Exit Criteria

- Session extraction workflow documented
- Knowledge generation workflow documented
- Template-driven knowledge generation process defined
- At least five historical sessions reviewed using PLB.002
- At least ten new knowledge objects created through session extraction
- Session-to-Knowledge process validated and refined

---

## Phase 3 – Knowledge Governance

### Status

Not Started

### Objective

Establish repeatable governance and quality assurance for the knowledge layer.

### Exit Criteria

- PLB.004 Knowledge Governance Review exists
- Governance review workflow documented
- Relationship review process documented
- Consistency review process documented
- First governance review executed
- First consistency review executed
- First relationship review executed
- Governance findings classification adopted

---

## Phase 4 – Context Intelligence

### Status

Not Started

### Objective

Transform the knowledge layer into a reusable AI context source.

### Exit Criteria

- Context Pack concept documented
- Context Pack template defined
- Knowledge selection rules documented
- Context assembly workflow documented
- At least three context packs created and tested
- Context packs used successfully in engineering sessions
