## Status

Active

## Constraint

Knowledge artifacts must remain structured and machine-readable.

## Rationale

The primary long-term consumer of the knowledge layer is AI-assisted reasoning.

Knowledge should therefore be optimized for retrieval, interpretation and context assembly.

## Implications

Knowledge objects should provide:

- stable identifiers
- explicit relationships
- consistent structure
- ownership information
- review status
