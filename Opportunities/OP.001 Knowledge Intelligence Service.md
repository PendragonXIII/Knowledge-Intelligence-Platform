## Status

Proposed

## Problem

Knowledge Objects are currently managed manually.

Creation, maintenance, validation and relationship management require significant manual effort.

As the Knowledge Repository grows, maintaining consistency, governance compliance and relationship integrity becomes increasingly difficult.

AI systems currently lack a structured interface for interacting with the Knowledge Repository.

## Opportunity

Create a Knowledge Intelligence Service that provides governed access to the Knowledge Repository.

The service shall support:

- knowledge retrieval
- knowledge search
- knowledge object creation
- knowledge object updates
- knowledge validation
- relationship discovery
- relationship validation
- context assembly
- governance reviews
- repository consistency analysis

The service shall act as the operational interface between AI agents and the Knowledge Repository.

## Expected Benefits

- reduced knowledge management effort
- improved governance compliance
- improved relationship quality
- reusable AI context
- higher recommendation consistency
- improved repository scalability
- automated knowledge maintenance
- improved engineering effectiveness

## Success Criteria

- AI systems can retrieve Knowledge Objects through a standardized interface
- AI systems can create governed Knowledge Objects
- Knowledge Objects can be validated automatically
- Relationship recommendations can be generated automatically
- Governance reviews can be partially automated
- Context Packs can be assembled automatically
