## Status

In Progress

## Problem

Every AI session requires manual context assembly.

Relevant knowledge is often forgotten or omitted.

## Opportunity

Automatically generate context packs tailored to a specific engineering task.

Potential context sources include:

- Capabilities
- Constraints
- Opportunities
- Learnings
- Architecture Decisions
- Repository Intelligence

## Expected Benefits

- reduced setup effort
- improved recommendation quality
- greater consistency across sessions

## Success Criteria

AI assistants can receive structured context packs with minimal manual preparation.
