## Status

Proposed

## Problem

Engineering prioritization and roadmap decisions are largely manual.

Decision quality depends heavily on individual context awareness.

## Opportunity

Provide AI-assisted engineering decision support based on:

- repository context
- capability maturity
- opportunities
- quality signals
- validation results
- architectural constraints

## Expected Benefits

- improved prioritization
- better investment decisions
- increased engineering effectiveness

## Success Criteria

Engineering decisions can be supported by structured evidence and automated recommendations.
