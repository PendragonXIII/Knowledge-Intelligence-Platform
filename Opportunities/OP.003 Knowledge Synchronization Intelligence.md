## Status

In Progress

## Problem

Repository implementation and knowledge artifacts may evolve independently.

This creates the risk of outdated or inconsistent knowledge.

## Opportunity

Provide automated synchronization and consistency analysis between:

- Repository
- Architecture
- Capability Catalog
- Knowledge Layer

## Expected Benefits

- reduced documentation drift
- improved governance quality
- increased trust in knowledge artifacts

## Success Criteria

Synchronization reviews identify inconsistencies and missing updates.
