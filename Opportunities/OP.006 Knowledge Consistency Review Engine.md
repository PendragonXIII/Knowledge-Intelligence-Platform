## Status

In Progress

## Problem

Knowledge artifacts can become outdated, incomplete or inconsistent over time.

As the knowledge layer grows, manual reviews become increasingly difficult.

## Opportunity

Provide automated consistency reviews for the knowledge layer.

Review areas include:

- missing relationships
- orphaned nodes
- outdated information
- duplicate concepts
- conflicting information
- missing ownership

## Expected Benefits

- improved knowledge quality
- reduced maintenance effort
- increased trust in knowledge artifacts
- improved AI context quality

## Success Criteria

Consistency reviews identify actionable findings and governance recommendations.
