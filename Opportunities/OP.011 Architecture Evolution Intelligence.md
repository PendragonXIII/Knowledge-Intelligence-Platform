## Status

Proposed

## Problem

Architectural evolution is largely reactive and dependent on manual observation.

Emerging structural issues may not be identified early.

## Opportunity

Provide AI-assisted architectural evolution analysis.

Potential analysis areas include:

- capability growth patterns
- structural imbalances
- domain boundaries
- architectural refactoring opportunities
- long-term maintainability

## Expected Benefits

- improved architectural sustainability
- earlier identification of structural issues
- better long-term planning
- reduced architectural debt

## Success Criteria

Architecture reviews generate actionable recommendations for future evolution.
