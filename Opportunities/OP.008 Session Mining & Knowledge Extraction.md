## Status

Proposed

## Problem

Valuable knowledge generated during AI sessions is frequently lost or requires significant manual effort to preserve.

## Opportunity

Automatically extract durable knowledge from AI sessions.

Potential outputs include:

- Learnings
- Constraints
- Opportunities
- ADRs
- Architecture Notes
- Playbooks

## Expected Benefits

- reduced documentation effort
- increased knowledge retention
- accelerated knowledge layer growth
- improved engineering effectiveness

## Success Criteria

AI sessions can be converted into structured knowledge objects with minimal manual effort.
