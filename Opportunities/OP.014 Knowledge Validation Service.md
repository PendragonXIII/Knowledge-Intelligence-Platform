## Status

Proposed

## Problem

Knowledge governance compliance is currently reviewed manually.

As repository size increases, manual validation becomes increasingly difficult and inconsistent.

## Opportunity

Create a Knowledge Validation Service that automatically validates Knowledge Objects against governance standards.

The service shall support:

- KTS compliance validation
- constraint compliance validation
- relationship validation
- identifier validation
- governance review support

## Expected Benefits

- improved governance compliance
- improved repository consistency
- reduced manual review effort
- improved knowledge quality

## Success Criteria

- Governance violations can be identified automatically
- Missing standards can be identified
- Missing relationships can be identified
- Validation reports can be generated
