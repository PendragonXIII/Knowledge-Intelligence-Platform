## Status

In Progress

## Problem

As the knowledge graph grows, it becomes increasingly difficult to understand its structure and identify weaknesses.

## Opportunity

Provide graph-level analysis of the knowledge layer.

Potential analysis includes:

- isolated nodes
- highly connected nodes
- knowledge clusters
- missing connections
- structural gaps

## Expected Benefits

- improved graph quality
- better discoverability
- stronger context assembly
- improved governance visibility

## Success Criteria

Graph analysis provides actionable recommendations for improving the knowledge layer.
