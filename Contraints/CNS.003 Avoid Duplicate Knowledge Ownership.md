## Status

Active

## Constraint

Each knowledge artifact must have a single authoritative owner.

## Rationale

Duplicate ownership creates inconsistency, conflicting information and loss of trust.

## Implications

- Repository owns implementation knowledge.
- Knowledge Layer owns reasoning knowledge.
- Operational systems own operational knowledge.
- Information should not be maintained in multiple locations.
