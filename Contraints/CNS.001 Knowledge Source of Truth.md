## Status

Active

## Purpose

Define authoritative ownership of engineering knowledge.

## Constraint

Operational data belongs to operational systems.

Implementation knowledge belongs to the repository.

Reasoning knowledge belongs to the knowledge layer.

## Rationale

Avoid duplicate ownership and conflicting information.

## Implications

- Repository remains source of truth for code and architecture.
- Knowledge Layer becomes source of truth for reasoning artifacts.
- Google Sheets remains source of truth for operational data.
