# Garden Capability Relationship Assessment V1

## Purpose

Establish an initial capability relationship model for the Garden Solution based on legacy capability analysis.

This assessment represents Version 1 and is intended as a foundation for future relationship refinement.

---

# Garden Capability Portfolio

## Foundation Layer

- Garden Knowledge Graph
- Observation Intelligence
- Plant Lifecycle & Data Maintenance

## Intelligence Layer

- Semantic Query & Reasoning
- Seasonal Intelligence
- Recommendation Engine

## Strategy Layer

- Garden Planning Intelligence
- External Knowledge Integration

## Governance Layer

- Governance & Data Quality

## Vision Layer

- Agentic Garden Operations

## Key Insight

Garden Knowledge Graph is the central knowledge system.

Observation Intelligence is the primary knowledge producer.

Agentic Garden Operations is the primary knowledge consumer.

This assessment should be reviewed after capability decomposition, opportunity extraction and architecture reconstruction activities are completed.
