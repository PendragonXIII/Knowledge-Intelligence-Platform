# PLB.001 Capability Governance Review

## Purpose

Provide a repeatable process for assessing, evolving, prioritizing and governing capabilities.

## Preconditions

- Relevant capability exists or is proposed
- Capability Governance is available
- Relevant ADRs are available
- Relevant Roadmaps are available

## Process

### Step 1 – Load Capability Context

Load:

- Capability Definition
- Capability Governance
- Related ADRs
- Related Roadmaps
- Related Opportunities
- Related Constraints

### Step 2 – Assess Capability State

Evaluate:

- Maturity
- Priority
- Strategic Leverage
- Agentic Relevance
- Current Bottlenecks

### Step 3 – Review Relationships

Validate relationships to:

- Opportunities
- Constraints
- Roadmaps
- ADRs
- Related Capabilities

### Step 4 – Identify Gaps

Identify:

- Missing capabilities
- Missing relationships
- Missing governance
- Missing roadmap alignment
- Ownership ambiguities

### Step 5 – Evaluate Evolution Options

Determine:

- Maintain
- Enhance
- Split
- Consolidate
- Retire
- Migrate

### Step 6 – Make Recommendation

Provide:

- Findings
- Recommended actions
- Governance implications
- Roadmap implications

## Success Criteria

Capabilities are:

- governed
- prioritized
- traceable
- roadmap-aligned
- evolution-ready
