## Purpose

Review the health, consistency and governance quality of the Knowledge Layer.

## Review Frequency

- After major architecture sessions
- After capability restructuring
- Before roadmap updates
- Before major AI workflow changes

## Review Areas

### Knowledge Object Compliance

Verify compliance with:

[[CNS.005 Knowledge Object Standards]]

### Relationship Quality

Identify:

- orphaned nodes
- weakly connected nodes
- duplicate relationships
- missing relationships

### Governance Consistency

Identify:

- conflicting knowledge objects
- outdated information
- duplicated ownership
- maturity inconsistencies

### Capability Coverage

Identify:

- opportunities without capabilities
- capabilities without opportunities
- capabilities without outcomes
- capabilities without constraints

### Roadmap Alignment

Verify alignment between:

- Opportunities
- Capabilities
- Roadmaps
- Architecture Decisions

## Review Output

Findings shall be classified as:

- DOCUMENTATION_GAP
- DOCUMENTATION_OUTDATED
- DOCUMENTATION_CONFLICT
- DOCUMENTATION_DUPLICATION
- DOCUMENTATION_FRAGMENTATION
- MATURITY_MISMATCH
- IMPLEMENTATION_MISSING
- PLANNED_CAPABILITY
