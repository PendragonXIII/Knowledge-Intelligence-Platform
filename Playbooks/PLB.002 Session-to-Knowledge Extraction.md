## Purpose

Convert valuable AI sessions into reusable knowledge objects.

## Process

1. Review session outcomes.
2. Identify durable insights.
3. Classify findings as:
   - Learning
   - Constraint
   - Opportunity
   - Architecture Decision
4. Create knowledge objects.
5. Create relationships.
6. Review graph consistency.

## Success Criteria

Knowledge generated during sessions becomes:

- reusable
- discoverable
- linkable
- available for future AI context assembly
