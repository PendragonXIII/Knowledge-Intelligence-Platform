## Standard

[[KTS.001 Capability Standard]]

## Purpose

Build and maintain an AI-consumable knowledge layer that improves context quality, recommendation consistency, governance maturity and engineering effectiveness.

## Current State

Operational

## North Star

Provide a self-maintaining, AI-consumable knowledge layer that continuously improves context quality, governance consistency, decision support and AI effectiveness across the Garden Solution ecosystem.

## Agentic Contribution

AI agents can:

- create knowledge objects
- update knowledge objects
- validate knowledge objects
- discover knowledge relationships
- identify inconsistencies
- recommend governance improvements
- assemble context packs
- extract knowledge from conversations
- synchronize knowledge structures
- support engineering decision making

## Outcomes

### O1 – Knowledge Object Management

Establish the ability to:

- create knowledge objects
- update knowledge objects
- maintain knowledge objects
- retire obsolete knowledge objects

Supported object types include:

- Capabilities
- Engineering Components
- Opportunities
- Constraints
- Learnings
- ADRs
- Playbooks
- Roadmaps

### O2 – Knowledge Validation

Establish the ability to validate knowledge objects against:

- CON constraints
- KTS standards
- governance rules
- relationship requirements
- consistency requirements

### O3 – Knowledge Relationship Intelligence

Establish the ability to:

- discover relationships
- recommend relationships
- validate relationships
- identify orphaned knowledge objects
- improve graph connectivity

### O4 – Knowledge Extraction

Establish the ability to:

- extract knowledge from AI sessions
- identify learnings
- identify opportunities
- identify decisions
- generate knowledge objects

### O5 – Context Assembly

Establish the ability to:

- build context packs
- assemble targeted AI context
- improve recommendation quality
- improve context relevance
- reduce prompt engineering effort

### O6 – Knowledge Governance

Establish the ability to:

- enforce standards
- monitor compliance
- execute governance reviews
- identify governance gaps
- recommend governance improvements

### O7 – Knowledge Synchronization

Establish the ability to synchronize:

- Repository Intelligence
- Capability Intelligence
- Governance Intelligence
- Documentation
- Knowledge Objects

### O8 – Engineering Decision Support

Establish the ability to:

- support capability planning
- support prioritization
- support architecture decisions
- support roadmap development
- support opportunity management

### O9 – Knowledge Intelligence Services

Establish services that provide structured access to the Knowledge Layer.

Services may support:

- knowledge retrieval
- knowledge validation
- relationship analysis
- context assembly
- knowledge object creation
- knowledge object maintenance

Knowledge Intelligence Services provide the operational interface between AI agents and the Knowledge Repository.
