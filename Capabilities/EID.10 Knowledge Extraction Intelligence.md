## Standard

[[KTS.001 Capability Standard]]

## Purpose

Extract structured knowledge candidates from repositories, engineering artifacts, governance documents, conversations and other knowledge sources to support knowledge creation, maintenance and governance automation.

## Current State

Operational

## North Star

Provide the ability for AI agents to continuously identify, extract and structure knowledge from unstructured and semi-structured information sources.

## Agentic Contribution

AI agents can:

- identify capabilities
- identify sub-capabilities
- identify constraints
- identify opportunities
- identify learnings
- identify architectural decisions
- identify roadmap items
- identify governance gaps
- identify knowledge relationships
- recommend knowledge updates

## Outcomes

Establish the ability to:

- identify capabilities

- identify capability candidates
- identify capability enhancements

### O2 – Constraint Extraction

Establish the ability to:

### O3 – Opportunity Extraction

### O4 – Learning Extraction

### O5 – Architectural Decision Extraction

Establish the ability to:

### O6 – Relationship Extraction

Establish the ability to:

Establish the ability to:

- analyze repository documents

Establish the ability to:

### O9 – Knowledge Candidate Generation

Establish the ability to generate structured knowledge candidates for review before repository modification.
