## Purpose

Define the standard structure and governance requirements for Learning knowledge objects.

## Mandatory Fields

- Observation
- Learning
- Related

## Recommended Fields

- Implications
- Recommended Actions

## Optional Fields

- Evidence
- Confidence Level

## Validation Rules

### V1

Every Learning must have a unique identifier.

### V2

Every Learning must follow KTS.004.

### V3

Every Learning should maintain at least three meaningful relationships.

### V4

Mandatory fields must be present.

### V5

Recommended fields should be completed whenever sufficient information exists.

### V6

Optional fields may be omitted.
