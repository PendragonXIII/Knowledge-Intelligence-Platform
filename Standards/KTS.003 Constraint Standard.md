## Purpose

Define the standard structure and governance requirements for Constraint knowledge objects.

## Mandatory Fields

- Status
- Constraint
- Rationale
- Requirements
- Related

## Recommended Fields

- Applicable Knowledge Objects
- Implications

## Optional Fields

- Exceptions
- Governance Notes

## Validation Rules

### V1

Every Constraint must have a unique identifier.

### V2

Every Constraint must follow KTS.003.

### V3

Every Constraint should maintain at least three meaningful relationships.

### V4

Mandatory fields must be present.

### V5

Recommended fields should be completed whenever sufficient information exists.

### V6

Optional fields may be omitted.
