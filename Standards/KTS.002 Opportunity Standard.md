## Purpose

Define the standard structure and governance requirements for Opportunity knowledge objects.

## Mandatory Fields

- Status
- Problem
- Opportunity
- Expected Benefits
- Related

## Recommended Fields

- Success Criteria
- Related Capabilities
- Related Constraints

## Optional Fields

- Priority
- Effort Estimate
- Dependencies

## Validation Rules

### V1

Every Opportunity must have a unique identifier.

### V2

Every Opportunity must follow KTS.002.

### V3

Every Opportunity must reference at least one related Capability.

### V4

Every Opportunity should maintain at least three meaningful relationships.

### V5

Mandatory fields must be present.

### V6

Recommended fields should be completed whenever sufficient information exists.

### V7

Optional fields may be omitted.
