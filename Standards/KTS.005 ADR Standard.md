## Purpose

Define the standard structure and governance requirements for Architecture Decision Record (ADR) knowledge objects.

## Mandatory Fields

- Status
- Context
- Decision
- Rationale
- Implications
- Related

## Recommended Fields

- Alternatives Considered
- Consequences
- Related Capabilities

## Optional Fields

- Superseded By
- Supersedes
- Decision Date

## Validation Rules

### V1

Every ADR must have a unique identifier.

### V2

Every ADR must follow KTS.005.

### V3

Every ADR should maintain at least three meaningful relationships.

### V4

Mandatory fields must be present.

### V5

Recommended fields should be completed whenever sufficient information exists.

### V6

Optional fields may be omitted.
