## Purpose

Define the standard structure and governance requirements for Roadmap knowledge objects.

## Mandatory Fields

- Purpose
- Phases
- Related

## Recommended Fields

- Vision
- Current Assessment
- Guiding Principles

## Optional Fields

- Risks
- Dependencies
- Assumptions

## Validation Rules

### V1

Every Roadmap must have a unique identifier.

### V2

Every Roadmap must follow KTS.007.

### V3

Every Roadmap phase must define:
- Status
- Objective
- Exit Criteria

### V4

Every Roadmap should maintain at least three meaningful relationships.

### V5

Mandatory fields must be present.

### V6

Recommended fields should be completed whenever sufficient information exists.

### V7

Optional fields may be omitted.
