# KTS.011 Engineering Handoff Standard

## Purpose

Define the structure and governance requirements for Engineering Handoffs.

Engineering Handoffs provide the formal transition between Knowledge Work and Engineering Work.

They capture implementation intent, architectural decisions and acceptance expectations before engineering execution begins.

---

## Mandatory Fields

- ID
- Title
- Capability
- Objective
- Scope
- Recommendation
- Architecture Decision
- Risks
- Acceptance Criteria

---

## Recommended Fields

- Dependencies
- Impact Assessment
- Validation Strategy
- Open Questions
- Related Learnings

---

## Optional Fields

- Alternatives Considered
- Roadmap References
- Future Evolution Notes

---

## Validation Rules

### V1
Engineering Handoffs must possess a unique identifier.

### V2
Engineering Handoffs must describe what should be implemented, not how it is coded.

### V3
Engineering Handoffs must contain acceptance criteria.

### V4
Engineering Handoffs must contain an explicit recommendation.

### V5
Engineering Handoffs should reference relevant capabilities and governance artifacts.
